LEGAL COMMUNICATION

Ref: OMJ-982/LC71/ยง256

Case No. 1798-CV-506

December 14, 2025

RE: BUTTON STATE ON KULOKET IS UNCLEAR

ADDRESSED TO: Student Graduaat Odisee

**OFFICE OF THE DEAN OF STUDENT AFFAIRS**
**Odisee University College**
**In re: Button State on KULoket (Case No. 2023-ODI-42)**

1. Whereas, pursuant to **Article 7.12(b)** of the *Flemish Higher Education Interface Accessibility Act (FHEIAA)*, all digital administrative portals must maintain "unambiguous and conspicuously labeled interactive elements" (hereinafter referred to as "buttons"); and whereas the petitioner has alleged, heretofore, that the current button state fails to meet the statutory clarity requirements under **Subsection 3.2.1** of the *Decree on Pedagogical Ergonomics* (2019), this office hereby issues the following response.

2. Be it resolved that, in accordance with **Precedent 42-ULB v. VUB** (2021), wherein the Court of Administrative Widgets held that "a button's opacity shall not be less than 70% nor its labeling more than 12 words," the current KULoket interface is hereby deemed *prima facie* non-compliant. Furthermore, the petitioner's ancillary demand for a "button that brings [them] drinks" is noted but dismissed under **Clause 9(f)** of the *Beverage Provision Limitation Statute*, which explicitly prohibits "the commingling of hydration services with academic administrative interfaces."

3. Wherefore, this office shall direct the KULoket Technical Oversight Committee (KTOC) to remediate the button's state within **thirty (30) calendar days**, pursuant to **Section 45(c)** of the *Digital Usability Enforcement Guidelines*. Failure to comply may result in sanctions under **Odisee Policy 12.8: "You Had One Job"**, including but not limited to mandatory sensitivity training for UI designers.

Respectfully submitted,

**Prof. Dr. J. Van Den Legalese**
Dean of Student Affairs
*Odisee University College*

**CC:** KTOC, Flemish Ministry of Education (Beverage Division)
